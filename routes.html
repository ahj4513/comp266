Small Note before presenting routes: If you wish to strictly follow the GPX routes, you may want to turn off automatic rerouting from your cycling computer. When I was doing my Grand Slam, the auto-routing sometimes added to confusion when the physical routing was not clear (which happens.. believe me).

4 River Cycling Tour

The 4 Rivers Cycling Tour actually consists of 6 segments! This is because Hangang, or Han River, is broken down into multiple segments for certification purposes. Complete these beautiful river segments by following the Strava routes below:

  1. HANGANG 4 Rivers Cycling Tour Route
  2. NAKDONGGANG 4 Rivers Cycling Tour Route
  3. GEUMGANG 4 Rivers Cycling Tour Route
  4. YEONGSANGANG 4 Rivers Cycling Tour Route

Grand Slam Cycling Tour

Grand Slam Cycling Tour requires a lot of effort. Completing this tour means you have dedication and willpower to endure almost a month of daily long-distance cycling. Use the routes given in above sections to gather all stamps. Good luck!
