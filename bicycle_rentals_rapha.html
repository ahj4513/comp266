Rapha Bicycle Rentals

If you are a Rapha cycling club member, you can take advantage of the bike rental system. You can rent a "Factor Ostro" (premium bike) for 3 days maximum. The rental can be booked 3-90 days in advance of the rental date, with cancellation allowed up to 24 hours before the collection time. Based on the reservation system check made on 20 March 2025, the maximum rental duration is 3 days.

The cost of the rental is ₤55 per day (in equivalent local currency). As of 20 Mar 2025, ₤55 converts to ₩104,445.
